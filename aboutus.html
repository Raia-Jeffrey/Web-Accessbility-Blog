This is a personal website/blog about all things coffee and espresso


I created this blog as a way of showing my knowledge and love of coffee.
after many years of barista'ing and consuming caffinated beverages.


I first started making coffee drinks when I joined my highschools IT department.
they were housed inside the library which had a break room us students could use.
My fellow IT students and I naturally chose to hang out there.
There was both a coffee and espresso machine and the rest is history.
